Optimizing Mental Health & Wellness for a Happier, Healthier Life

Prioritizing mental health is key to a balanced life. TheraCounsel offers expert therapy and counseling for anxiety, depression, and trauma through telehealth and in-person sessions, guiding individuals toward healing and personal growth.

Transform Your Life with Expert Mental Health Support

TheraCounsel is a dedicated team of compassionate mental health specialists, including psychotherapists and psychiatric mental health nurse practitioners (PMHNPs). We provide personalized therapy for depression, anxiety, trauma, and life challenges, guiding you toward healing and personal growth through telehealth and in-person counseling.

Who We Are

  • Psychotherapists and Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioners (PMHNPs) with a passion to make a difference in the lives of others
  • Specialists in treating a variety of mental and behavioral issues including: depression, anxiety, grief, loss, trauma and coping with everyday life challenges
  • Clinicians who meet with you in the comforts and convenience of your own environment through telehealth or in person in an office setting

What We Do

  • One to one needs assessments, with history taking and evidence-based evaluation and assessment tools
  • Establishing baseline and desired treatment results
  • Unique and individualized treatment planning with specific, measurable, attainable realistic, time framed treatment goals and objectives

Why It Matters

  • Adopt new ways of thinking and behaving
  • Become who you were meant to be
  • Cultivate healthier, healthier relationships
  • Develop creative personal solutions
  • Enhance life satisfaction
  • Live a more wholehearted life
