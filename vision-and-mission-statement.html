TheraCounsel Compassionate Mental Health & Teletherapy Services

TheraCounsel provides expert psychotherapy through telehealth and in-office sessions. Our dedicated mental and behavioral health specialists offer compassionate support for emotional well-being.

Our Mission

At TheraCounsel, we believe that not all wounds are visible. Our team of licensed mental health professionals, including Licensed Clinical Social Workers (LCSWs) and Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioners (PMHNPs), is dedicated to guiding individuals on a transformative journey toward emotional well-being. We provide expert psychotherapy through secure telehealth therapy sessions and compassionate in-office counseling. Our goal is to help clients heal their hearts and minds by:

  • Creating a safe, confidential, and supportive space for mental health healing
  • Helping individuals overcome anxiety, depression, trauma, and emotional distress
  • Using evidence-based therapies tailored to each person's unique mental health needs
  • Empowering clients to achieve lasting personal growth, self-awareness, and resilience

Whether you seek individual therapy, couples counseling, or mental health support, TheraCounsel is here to walk with you every step of the way.
