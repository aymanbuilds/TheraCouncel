Meet the TheraCounsel Team: Compassionate Mental Health Experts

At TheraCounsel, our dedicated mental health specialists, psychotherapists, and Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioners (PMHNPs) are committed to helping individuals overcome anxiety, depression, trauma, and life's challenges. Whether through telehealth therapy or in-person counseling, our team provides personalized, evidence-based care in a safe and supportive environment, empowering you on your journey to mental wellness.

Personalized Mental Health Care for Growth and Healing

At TheraCounsel, we provide expert mental health care tailored to your needs. Whether you're looking for experienced therapists, flexible session options, or the lasting impact of psychotherapy, we offer compassionate support through both in-person and telehealth sessions. Our goal is to help you navigate challenges, foster resilience, and enhance emotional well-being.

How to Move Forward?

If you are ready to be the person you were meant to be, check your insurance to make sure we are an in-network provider and contact us to set up an evaluation with our next available mental health specialist. We are in-network with most major insurances, including Medicare, Medicaid, HMOs, PPOs, and commercial insurances.

Where Are Sessions Provided?

  • We provide sessions in person sessions in an office location at 4546 N Federal Hwy Fort Lauderdale fl 33305
  • We provide virtual/telehealth sessions where you meet with a TheraCounsel professional from the comforts and convenience of your own home environment.

How does Individual Psychotherapy Impact Others?

  • Increased empathy for oneself and others,
  • Strengthened community connections and communications
  • Increased social skills, social participation, collaboration
