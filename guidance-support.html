Understanding the Impact of Depression and How TheraCounsel Helps You Save on Healthcare Costs

At TheraCounsel we help clients live better lives, while saving health care dollars. Depression, for example, can have multiple physical manifestations, and appear to be a whole host of different disorders, triggering unnecessary and costly treatment. Norman Geschwind, Chief of Neurology at Harvard, often lectured on how depression could mimic almost any neurological disorder – down to “pathognomon” physical stigma of stroke often resulting in unnecessary hospitalization and readmissions.
